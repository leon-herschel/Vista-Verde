How to get there

To get to Vista Verde from Cebu City, you can take a bus or jeepney bound for Ayala Center Cebu, and then transfer to a 25C or 22I bus or jeepney that will take you to Tintay. From there, you can ride a tricycle or motorcycle to the resort. The travel time can vary depending on traffic, but it's usually around 30 minutes from Ayala Center Cebu to Vista Verde.

Alternatively, you can catch a V-hire or Bus from SM City Cebu Terminal which will take you directly to Consolacion Public Market. Once you arrive at the market, you need to take a tricycle or motorcycle ride to reach the resort. The usual fare ranges from PHP 20 to PHP 30 per person for a tricycle ride and PHP 50 to PHP 70 for a motorcycle ride.

Overall, getting to Vista Verde is easy and accessible, and the beautiful natural surroundings make the journey just as enjoyable as the destination.
