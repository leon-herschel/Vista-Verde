A great place to enjoy life

Vista Verde offers you the opportunity to indulge in a lifestyle of privilege and satisfaction. With its prime location, this exquisite development provides both accessibility to city landmarks and the privacy of an exclusive community. Moreover, Vista Verde is a perfect retreat for those seeking solitude and tranquility, away from the hustle and bustle of city life. The peaceful environment, coupled with breathtaking views of Mactan Island, creates an idyllic setting for relaxation and rejuvenation.

Enjoy the cool gentle breeze from the mountains and unwind with the relaxing sound of the chirping of birds or your children's laughter away from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Nestle within the comfort of a relaxing resort with an overlooking view of majestic mountains and natural landscape, accompanied by an array of top-notch amenities.
